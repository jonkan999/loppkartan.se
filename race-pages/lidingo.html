Om loppet:

All info på den här sidan är baserat på öppen källdata. Vi gör vårt bästa för att hålla den uppdaterad men kan inte garantera att allt är korrekt. Ser du några felaktigheter, kontakta oss gärna kontakt så rättar vi till det snarast.

Summer is coming and we are excited to announce the opening of registration for the IFK Lidingö Friidrottsklubb Sommaridrottsskola! The race will feature several categories for runners of all ages and skill levels, including the 1500 meter and 3000 meter distances. The race will take place on our state-of-the-art track, providing runners with the perfect environment to test their speed and endurance. Whether you're a seasoned runner looking to challenge yourself or a beginner wanting to experience the thrill of competitive racing, the IFK Lidingö Friidrottsklubb Sommaridrottsskola has something for everyone. Our race director and staff are dedicated to providing a safe and enjoyable race experience for all participants. So lace up your running shoes, bring your competitive spirit, and join us for an exciting day of racing at our track. Bokningen öppen till årets Sommaridrottsskola!
