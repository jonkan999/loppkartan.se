Om loppet:

All info på den här sidan är baserat på öppen källdata. Vi gör vårt bästa för att hålla den uppdaterad men kan inte garantera att allt är korrekt. Ser du några felaktigheter, kontakta oss gärna kontakt så rättar vi till det snarast.

Join us for the exciting Mölndalsloppet on September 3rd, 2023! We invite you to run or walk with us as we set Mölndal in motion. Gather your family and friends and make this day the highlight of your year! Whether you're running solo or challenging your friends to a relay, Mölndalsloppet 2023 has a race for everyone. The race will take place alongside Sports Day, so it's sure to be an action-packed day of fun and fitness. With race categories including a Short Race of 850 meters and a Middle Race of 5 kilometers, there's something for runners of all levels. Lace up your shoes and get ready to hit the road as we take on Mölndalsloppet! Sign up now and be part of the excitement.
