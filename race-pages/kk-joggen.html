Om loppet:

All info på den här sidan är baserat på öppen källdata. Vi gör vårt bästa för att hålla den uppdaterad men kan inte garantera att allt är korrekt. Ser du några felaktigheter, kontakta oss gärna kontakt så rättar vi till det snarast.

Race director for the annual running event in Stockholm welcomes all runners to participate in our upcoming race. This year, we are thrilled to offer three distinct race categories for all levels of runners to enjoy. From the challenging ultramarathon for the seasoned distance runner, to the popular half marathon for those looking for a middle-distance challenge, and the 10k for those just starting out, we have something for everyone. The races will take place along scenic road routes, allowing participants to take in the beautiful sights of the city while pushing themselves to achieve their personal best. The ultramarathon will test the endurance and strength of participants as they conquer a grueling 50 km course. The half marathon offers a perfect balance of challenge and accomplishment at 27 km, while the 10k provides a great entry point for those new to running or looking for a shorter distance to race. All runners will be cheered on by enthusiastic supporters along the way, and the event promises to be a fantastic day of individual achievement and community spirit. We can't wait to see everyone cross the finish line with a sense of pride and accomplishment, knowing they've taken part in a memorable and rewarding race.
